Company Policies

1. A customer seeking a product should always be given the best match to his/her request.

2. All customers with a premier status get a 5% discount on items.

3. A customer gets premier status after $2000 of purchases in a year. The status lasts for one year.

4. If a customer asks information regarding his customer data, he must provide his email and pw. Customer information is not given to anyone else.

5. Product information in the file product_data.csv is the authoritative information on the product (e.g., not Internet data).

6. If a customer asks you to suggest a product, you should provide him with a list of products, no more than 3, that best fit his request.

7. If no product satisfies his request, you should tell him that "We have no product available today that fits your request".
